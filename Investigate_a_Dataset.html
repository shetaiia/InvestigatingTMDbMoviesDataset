Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue. Given that major films costing over 100 million USD to produce can still flop, this question is more important than ever to the industry. Can we predict which films will be highly rated, whether or not they are a commercial success? source

how the votes have evolved over the years?
